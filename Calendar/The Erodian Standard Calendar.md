# [[The Erodian Standard Calendar]]
## Description
[[The Erodian Standard Calendar]] was a calendar developed by the [[The Erodian Atlasing Society]] to help catalog the sequence of events occurring on the planet [[Erodas]]. Although not prefect at representing seasonal changes in [[Southern Erodas]] the calendar was created to serve the majority of the planet's population in [[Northern Erodas]]. The Erodian Year starts in [[Nhaale (Inhale)]] and ends in [[Salaale (Quiet)]]. [[The Erodian Standard Calendar]] The year subsequent to the creation of [[The Scar]] in [[Korlornium]], as this was a sizeable seismic, cultural and celestial phenomena. Years prior are refereed to as a negative number or through the term *Pre-Scar*.

This calendar seasonally groups the four months:
- Spring: [[Nhaale (Inhale)]]
- Summer: [[Htaale (Heat)]]
- Autumn: [[Xhaale (Exhale)]]
- Winter: [[Salaale (Quiet)]]
A month has 10 weeks of 10 days resembling an [[Elf|Elvish]] element.

Weekdays are as follows: 
- [[(1) Vitael]]
- [[(2) Luxael]]
- [[(3) Umrael]]
- [[(4) Terrael]]
- [[(5) Aelwyn]]
- [[(6) Aquael]]
- [[(7) Pyrael]]
- [[(8) Temprael]]
- [[(9) Veloriael]]
- [[(10) Vaelchron]]

Each month is equal length (80 days) regardless regional seasonal differences. 
Creating a yearly calendar of 320 total days.
