# Feywild
The [[Feywild]] is a alternate plane of existence parallel to [[The Mundane World]]. 

There are plenty of gates to this alternate plane scattered across [[Erodas]]. 

The party may end up accessing the [[Feywild]] through a gate located in [[Heaven's Hole]].

The [[Feywild]] is broken up into seasonal courts:[[ The Spring Court]], [[The Summer Court]], [[The Fall Court]] and [[The Winter Court]].
