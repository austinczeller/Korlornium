# Torren's Shop
[[Torren's Shop]] is a armory run by the blacksmith [[Torren Roughskull]] in the town of [[Stormveil]].
It is located near at the edge of [[Stormveil]] in a shed off to the side of [[Torren Roughskull]]'s residence.
The shed is a large brown barn-like structure with open entryway and a large chimney going through the roof. 

Most of the business is selling new axes and saws to the [[Stormwood Mill]].
