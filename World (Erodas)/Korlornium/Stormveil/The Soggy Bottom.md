# The Soggy Bottom 

## Description
[[The Soggy Bottom]] is a tavern in [[Stormveil]]. 
The bar is a musty smelling, dark and dank open barn with wet dirt floors. On nice days they open the bar doors and let the place air out. Seating is an assortment of makeshift scrap wood nailed together with varying degrees of comfort.

There are no rooms attached to [[The Soggy Bottom]] but there are a few camp spots around back.
