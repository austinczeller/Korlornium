# Cloudfall Temple
[[Cloudfall Temple]] is a temple dedicated to [[Parsus]] located in [[Stormveil]].

The interior is made out of dark cherry [[Stormwood]] and stained glass. It is a spectacle to behold. There is a grand organ in the front of the temple which plays almost ethereal melodies. The main chamber is illuminated by a massive glass celling, and the rain resonates loudly throughout the chamber.
It gives a peaceful and meditative vibe.

[[Reverend Oscar Xun]] runs the temple.
