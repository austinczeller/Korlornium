# Scalecrest Bluffs
The [[Scalecrest Bluffs]] are a rocky foothills of the [[Agite Mountains]] located in the [[Pugent Delta]] of [[Korlornium]]. 
The area is considered to fairly dangerous with many sightings of [[Dragon]]s and wyrmling.
