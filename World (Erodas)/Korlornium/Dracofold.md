A vast continent ruled by [[Dragon|dragons]]. There is a small resilient human population typically reside near dragon settlements to provide trade. The dragons act as the authoritative powers in these lands and the people are their subjects/citizens.

It is said that [[Dragon|dragons]] were birthed from the rock and stone in the crater located in the center of the continent. The mother dragon, [[Tiamat]], was the first and most powerful creation.
