# Thalmyre
![[thalmyre.png]]
[[Thalmyre]] is a large mystical island in the center of [[The Scar]]. This island is said to be protected by the wrathful beasts born from the [[Tidemother]]. Occasionally brave pirates attempt a "Thalmyre Run" to find the bounties of [[The Dread Pirate, Captain Garrick Hollowmaw]]. 

The isle is controlled by the [[The Hydra (Syndicate)]], a piracy and crime syndicate who majorly controls shipping in [[Korlornium]].

The island is home to one large port city, [[Port Thalmyre]] but many of the pirate gangs have their own small communities throughout the island, who are often in armed conflict with one another.
