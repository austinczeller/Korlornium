# Widow's Rook
A large rocky cliff face about 2 kilometers off shore. Romantics often marvel at it's beauty from the shores of Sapphire Beach.

Notably this is where a wizard was hiding, causing [[Sea Rot]] and hoarding [[Spice]]. 

This was the last place anyone has seen [[Ira]].
