# Tidemother Temple of Duskwind
The Tidemother Temple of Duskwind is a very small temple run by [[Father Elias Vance]] located in [[Duskwind Landing]] near [[The Sunken Sail]] Tavern. 

The party found that the temple may be tied up with the [[Spice]] trade.
