# The General
The General is a general goods store run by the halfling [[Bert Coggins]].

A cluttered but well-organized shop filled with adventuring gear, supplies, and everyday necessities. The walls are lined with shelves stacked high with ropes, lanterns, bedrolls, and dusty old trinkets. The smell of oiled leather and sawdust lingers in the air.

Behind the counter, a massive locked cabinet houses anything Bert deems "too interesting to be left lying around." No one knows how deep his storage room goes.

### Notable Features:

- The Bargain Bin: A small chest near the door filled with oddities, damaged gear, and questionable "discounts."

- The Ledger: Bert records every purchase in an old leather book. He claims it’s for bookkeeping, but it’s suspiciously detailed.

- The "No Credit" Sign: A faded wooden sign reading: No credit. No tabs. No exceptions. The phrase "Except Horace" is carved underneath in smaller letters.
