# Duskwind Landing Town Hall
The [[Duskwind Landing Town Hall]] is the community and political center of [[Duskwind Landing]]. Currently there is nobody in the mayor position but [[Borin Duskrag]] and [[Shandra Loomens]] seem to have a joint relationship and understanding of how to run the town.
