# Korlornium
[[Korlornium]] is a small isolated continent in the greater world of [[Erodas]]. It is known to have a rich theological history, political strife, diverse ecology, and unique geological phenomena. The sparsely populated eastern side of the continent, [[Siroth]], is notably the birthplace of the intelligent species [[Fowlmen]]. The western side of the continent, [[Caldrith]], is more populous and home to several complex societies.
