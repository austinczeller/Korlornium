# Ossaline River
The [[Ossaline River]] is a large river in the [[Pugent Delta]] region of [[Korlornium]]. It runs from the [[Scalecrest Bluffs]] through [[Stormveil]]. The river is know to be perilous to cross as water levels fluctuate rapidly.
