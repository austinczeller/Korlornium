# Casa LeBlanc
 [[Casa LeBlanc]] is the residence of [[Madam LeBlanc]] within [[The Spring Court]] of the [[Feywild]].
 The portal in [[Heaven's Hole]] leads to the gardens of [[Casa LeBlanc]].
