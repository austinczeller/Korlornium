# **Heaven's Hole**
**Nestled within the [[Pugent Delta]], Heaven’s Hole is a place where the veil between [[The Mundane World]] and the [[Feywild]] is at its thinnest. At its heart lies a deep, iridescent pool—a mysterious gateway rumored to transport those who dare submerge themselves into the enchanting yet perilous [[Feywild]]. Nearby villages like [[Stormveil]] have a bad notion about the hole and warn children not to venture too far into the forest.**

## **The Landscape**

**The surrounding forest is a lush, emerald expanse, rich with hanging moss, towering ferns, and ancient trees whose bark shimmers faintly in the dim, misty air. The land is perpetually damp, the air thick with the scent of fresh rain and blooming flora. Waterfalls cascade from unseen heights, their melodic rush blending with the chorus of unseen creatures that call this enchanted place home.**
## **Heaven’s Hole – The Pool Itself**

**From afar, the waters of [[Heaven's Hole]] appear a striking cerulean blue, still and inviting. Yet as one draws near, the surface shimmers with an unearthly iridescence, reflecting more than just the sky—it seems to flicker with glimpses of another world. Some say they see floating motes of light, twisting shadows, or the faint outline of figures watching from below.**

**Beyond the depths of the pool lies the [[Feywild]], specifically the gardens of [[Casa LeBlanc]] where [[Madam LeBlanc]], quirky collector of culture, tales, and trinkets has taken up residence.** 

## **Dangers and Mysteries**

**While breathtakingly beautiful, [[Heaven's Hole]] is not without peril. Travelers report time slipping away strangely, with some emerging from the woods believing only hours have passed when days—or even weeks—have gone by. Others speak of illusions and whispers that lure the unwary toward the water’s edge, tempting them to enter without thought of return.**

**For those seeking to harness the power of [[Korlornium]]’s ley lines, [[Heaven's Hole]] is a site of great significance. However, whether it is a sacred passage or a trap laid by the Fey remains a mystery.**
