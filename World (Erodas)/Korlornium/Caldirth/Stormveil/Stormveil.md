# Stormveil
## Description

A sleepy rainy logging town in the [[Pugent Delta]] region of [[Caldrith]] in [[Korlornium]]. Their is folklore that the clouds in [[Stormveil]] are so thick they are seeing the underside of [[Nilah]] the water dragon others think this is the birthplace of [[Parsus]]. 
Streets are muddy and dirt roads with occasional horse drawn carts carrying timber and other goods.
