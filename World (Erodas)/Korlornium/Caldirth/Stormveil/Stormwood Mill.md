# Stormwood Mill
[[Stormwood Mill]] is the major industry for folks in [[Stormveil]]. It's main investor is a man named [[Terrance Highbrow]] who recently moved to town within the past 5 years from [[Dithoria City]].
At the mill [[Stormwood]] is processed and prepared for shipment around the world. Most of the shipments go to luxury builders in [[Dithoria City]] and [[Cragbrook]].

The mill is currently working on expanding their harvesting permits to [[Heaven's Hole]] where there seems to be a unique quality of [[Stormwood]].
