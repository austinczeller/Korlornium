# Frostcrag Glade
## Overview 
[[Frostcrag Glade]] is one of the largest mining towns in the [[Agite Mountains]]. 
It is currently under the rule of the wizard [[Viros]] who commands the town from a tall wizard tower.
[[Viros]] discovered a magical ore, [[Ulthite]], within the mines.
