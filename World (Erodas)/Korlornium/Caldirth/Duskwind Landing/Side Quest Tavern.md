# Side Quest Tavern

The Side Quest Tavern was a recently closed tavern located in South [[Duskwind Landing]], near the [[Rusty Hook]] and once in direct competition with [[The Sunken Sail]]. It was owned and operated by [[Kel]]’s family, and closed under his leadership following the mysterious disappearance of his parents.

## Conspiracies & Closure

Rumors surrounding the tavern’s closure are abundant. Some claim the family owed a significant debt to a piracy gang, while others say they were lost at sea. In truth, Kel’s parents made a deal with [[Piamat]], a fey entity who gifted them a cow, claiming it would be the key ingredient to perfecting their oatmeal. However, Piamat demanded a favor in the [[Feywild]] in return. Due to the distorted perception of time across planes, Kel’s parents have been missing for years, despite believing their task would be brief.

## The Tavern’s Unusual Menu

The tavern was notorious for serving only oatmeal and gruel, regardless of the time of day. They experimented with mixing ale and other liquids into their dried oatmeal but never found a satisfactory solution. Before their disappearance, Kel’s parents purchased a cow, adamant that its milk would be the perfect ingredient. This cow, however, was a harbinger of doom, its presence tied directly to their fey bargain.

## The Decor

The walls of the tavern are covered in colorful posters of various fantasy-themed cereal brands:
- A rosy-cheeked, red-haired gnome cheerfully holding a colorful bowl of fun-shaped oatmeal.
- A Fowlman with a vibrant, colorful beak presenting a bowl of multicolored circular oats.
- A rakshasa sporting a blue bandana, giving a big thumbs up next to flat, flaky oats.

## Potential Plot Hook

[[Kel]] is struggling to keep the tavern from falling into complete disrepair, while also dealing with his parents’ disappearance. The party may become involved in investigating the mystery, confronting the fey entity [[Piamat]], or even journeying to the [[Feywild]] to bring [[Kel]]’s parents back.
