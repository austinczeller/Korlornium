# Sapphire Beach
Sapphire Beach is a nice tourist beach in [[Duskwind Landing]]. It has a great view of [[Widow's Rook]] and is a popular spot for romantics to have a picnic.

[[Faefi]] noticed that the tides are particularly high here and nearly touching a gazebo. 

[[Duskwind Delights]] is located near on the beach.

You walk underneath tall metal signs read Sapphire Beach as you enter and exit the beach.
