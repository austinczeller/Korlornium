# Duskwind Delights
Duskwind Delights is an ice cream stand located on [[Sapphire Beach]]. 

In [[Session 1- Sea Rot]] the party broke into the shop and stole all the gold kept in the register. They also noticed how sticky it was and noted the rat poison surrounding the shop.
