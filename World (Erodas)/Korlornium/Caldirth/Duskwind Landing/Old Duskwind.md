# Old Duskwind
Old Duskwind the name given to the ruins on the outskirts of [[Duskwind Landing]].


In [[Session 2- The Call of the Ruins]] the party investigated strange noises emerging from the ruins which was reported by [[Melkor Fluffybottom]]. There are several glyphs of the earth god [[Ulthagos]] scattered around. [[The Salty Bitches]] discovered a library in the dungeon of one of a ruin of [[Ulthagos]] where they met [[Zora Meadowlark]]. In order to gain access to the library [[Vel'Korr]] gave in to the earth and communed with [[Ulthagos]].
