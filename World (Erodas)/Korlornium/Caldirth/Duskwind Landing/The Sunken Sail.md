# The Sunken Sail
A tavern owned and operated by [[Horace Greentusk]]. The only tourist friendly bar in town. The bar itself is a old ship which wrecked, and the bar is in the hull of the ship. 
A large hook is hung above the bar, a warning to those who whish to stir up trouble.

#### For Purchase
 **Drinks:**
- Duskwind Grog (5 cp) – A foul-smelling, strong house brew.
- _Blackwake Rum_ (10 cp) – A pirate favorite, burns on the way down.
- _Silvercrest Wine_ (1 sp) – One of the few decent things in town.
- _Mystery Shot_ (10 cp) – No one orders it twice.
 
 **Food:**
- _Salted fish and hardtack_ (5 cp) – Basic sailor fare.
- _Spiced crab stew_ (2 sp) – Surprisingly good, but sometimes questionable.
- _Meat pie_ (3 sp) – Contents unknown, but it’s filling.
- _Talia’s Special_ (Varies) – If she likes you, she’ll bring you something _actually_ good. If not… hope you like extra salt.
 
 **Extras:**
- **A Room for the Night** (5 sp) – Comes with a cot, a lockable chest, and a guarantee that Horace won’t rob you.
