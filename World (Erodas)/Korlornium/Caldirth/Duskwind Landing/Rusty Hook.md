# Rusty Hook
When you first enter the [[Rusty Hook]] you enter what appears to be a small bar dimly lit with nobody in it. Dust covers the counter tops and there is very generic posters of buccaneers and beer on the wall. 
Players need to find the entrance in order to enter the actual bar. 

PUZZLE:
There are three taps with a moon, fire, wave, and a skull.
Welcome to the Rusty Hook: speak thy name and may the god's eat my arse

> *Where you are going you need not light*   
> *Creation engulfs you as you find your might
> As it swells and surges  
> The huntress shall not find her mark today*  
> *And find the way from this old cage*

The trick is to have them pull the taps while speaking saying the gods name in the order of the riddle above
moon->[[Alune]]
fire-> [[Forgas]]
wave->[[Tidemother]]
skull-> [[Sibuna]]
rain drop-> [[Parsus]]


The [[Rusty Hook]] is a smokey bar with wood paneling.
dagger dart boards

[[Borin Duskrag]] is the owner. Seems to be a guild hall for a pirate band. 
Here you can buy ale and browse the job board.

[[Borin Duskrag]] wants to organize a WWE style fight in the fight pit. 

You have 3 rounds to earn as many style points as possible against the reigning champion
