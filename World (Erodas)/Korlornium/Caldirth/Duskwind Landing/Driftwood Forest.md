# Driftwood Forest
The Driftwood Forest is a forest on the outskirts of [[Duskwind Landing]]. It separates [[Old Duskwind]] from the modern day [[Duskwind Landing]]. 

It is used as a food gathering place grounds for many druids who live in the town. 

This is one of [[Soffalie]]'s favorite places to spend time.
