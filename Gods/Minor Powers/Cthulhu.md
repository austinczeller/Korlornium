# Cthulhu
Is an extraplanar kraken deity who has taken interest in [[The Mundane World]], specifically [[Korlornium]] due to features like [[The Scar]].

[[Picasso]] has pledged himself to this creature and is handing out pamphlets on his behalf.

[[Cthulhu]] has come to [[Picasso]] in his dreams appearing as [[Sliver]] and a white squid. In this vision [[Picasso]] saw the entity devour his cousins [[The Samurai Tortles]].
