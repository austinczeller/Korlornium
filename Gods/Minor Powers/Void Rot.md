# [[Void Rot]]
## Description
[[Void Rot]] is a corruption caused by the presence of unearthly living and divine, radioactive-like, ore on [[Erodas]].
[[Void Rot]] is slowly spreading throughout [[Erodas]] but the spread was significantly halted as [[Dithoria (God)]] sealed himself in the [[The Ethereal Chain]], with the help of [[Ulthagos]], who crafted the seal from his own flesh, and the [[Tidemother]] who eventually buried the seal underneath [[The Scar]]. 

As time continued [[Void Rot]] learned to spread in planes which the current gods had less powers mainly the [[The Underdark]]. 

[[Void Rot]]  is sentient and has a unyielding hunger and desire to spread like a fungus. It sends it's agents across [[Erodas]] to assist in this goal in whatever way they can.

Currently [[Void Rot]] has killed the god [[Dithoria (God)]] and is using it's corrupted form to influence [[Dithoria City|Dithorians]] to release it from its seal.

Currently [[Void Rot]] is only able to send it's agents to [[Erodas]] on the [[The Mundane World]] through portals from  [[The Underdark]].

Other powerful entities , nearing divinity status on [[Erodas]], such as [[The Queen of Ravens]] and [[Cthulhu]], see [[Void Rot]] as a powerful weapon to create a clean slate of gods and expand their powers to [[Erodas]].
