# The Queen of Ravens
[[The Queen of Ravens]] is a lesser deity who grants mortals visions of the future, she grants them the power to see their deaths.

She has many servants in across [[Erodas]] including [[Beak]]. In [[Korlornium]] she has a major temple at [[The World's Eye]] although there has been rumors about secret underground temples/places of worship in the holy city of [[Dithoria City]] as people need comfort in the trying times.
