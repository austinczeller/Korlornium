# [[The Pantheon of Major Powers]]
## Description
[[The Pantheon of Major Powers]] is the group of divine powers of current reign over [[The Mundane World]] and [[Erodas]].

"mortals" believe that the Gods are immortal but they are not just have an unfathomable lifespan. They still must pass their genes off. and be born anew.
they in particular will not be reborn but their duties will be passed on to the chosen offspring.
