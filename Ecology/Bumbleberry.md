# [[Bumbleberry]]
## Description
[[Bumbleberry|Bumbleberries]] are a purple berry that have a sweet taste. 
[[Owlbear]]'s are known to love them
[[Bumbleberry|Bumbleberries]] grow in moist environments around [[Korlornium]] but are primarily found in heavily forested areas in the [[Pugent Delta]].
