# One-trek
A [[One-trek]] is the right of passage which many [[The Community at Heaven's Hole| Community members of Heaven's Hole]] take part in. It typically involves leaving [[Heaven's Hole]] to have an experience or bring something back to the community. Upon completion of a [[One-trek]] the individual will descend into the [[Heaven's Hole]] pool to determine if they get the title of '*Deepseer*'. Individuals who cross the planar boundary to the [[Feywild]] receive this title and are often met by [[Madam LeBlanc]] who collects the tales of the [[One-trek]]. If an individual fails to cross the planar boundary they either perish or the highest ranking 'Deepseer' collects the story or object and delivers it to the [[Madam LeBlanc]] in their place.
