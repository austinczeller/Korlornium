# The 33rd Veil-Master Race

The 33rd mayoral race of the town [[Stormveil]]. 
Candidates include [[Terrance Highbrow]] and [[Numa]].
The winner was [[Numa]] with [[Rick Augerland]] as the vice-master with the support of [[The Miller's Union]].

[[The Salty Bitches]] helped the town conduct an election at [[The Soggy Bottom]] after an attack by doppelgangers occurred during [[Terrance Highbrow]]'s speech. [[The Salty Bitches]] suspected that [[Zora Meadowlark]] had some part in rigging the election and planning the attack to put blame on [[Heaven's Hole]]. 
## Hot topics
- [[Terrance Highbrow]] would like remove previous logging restrictions in the [[Heaven's Hole]] area. He has gained quick popularity as the town is looking to boost the economy. 
- The death of [[Damon Warthelm]]. Many folks are suspicious of the death of the previous Veilfather.
## Context
The previous Veilfather, [[Damon Warthelm]] was found dead in the [[Permitting Office of Stormveil]].
