# [[Tidescrest]]
A festival celebrated in [[Thalmyre]] where a tournament is held to recruit newcomers to various piracy gangs, and establish power dynamics among groups.
The main festivities occur on [[The Seat of Thalmyre]].
The holiday was originally created to celebrate the [[Tidemother]] but this meaning is lost for most common folk, as pirates usually aren't too faithful.
