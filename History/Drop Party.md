# Drop Party
A [[Drop Party]] is an event that happens occasionally in [[The Community at Heaven's Hole]] where the youth gather around to bet on where a chicken will shit.

### **Betting Options**

| Bet Type          | Description                                            | Payout | Example                    |
| ----------------- | ------------------------------------------------------ | ------ | -------------------------- |
| **Exact Square**  | Bet on a specific number (1–36).                       | 35:1   | “I put 5 gp on square 17!” |
| **Color Bet**     | Bet on **white** or **black** squares.                 | 1:1    | “5 gp on white!”           |
| **Row or Column** | Bet on which _row_ or _column_ the drop lands in.      | 5:1    | “I’ll take Row 3!”         |
| **Corner Bet**    | Bet on one of the four corner squares.                 | 8:1    | “Corner 1!”                |
| **Even/Odd**      | Bet on whether the winning number will be even or odd. | 1:1    | “Even for me!”             |
