# Sea Rot
Sea Rot is disease which was plaguing the town of [[Duskwind Landing]]. 
The Sea Rot caused sailors who left the harbor to fall sick out at sea and never return. A wizard who was hiding on [[Widow's Rook]] was responsible for this disease/curse and was hoarding the [[Spice]] shipments.

After some guidance from a traveling group of [[Gnome|gnomes]] in [[Driftwood Forest]], [[Faefi]], [[Picasso]], [[Ira]], [[Vel'Korr]], and [[Soffalie]] cleansed the town of the aliment.
