# Sea Rot

The Sea Rot was a curse afflicting [[Duskwind Landing]] — sailors who left the harbour returned with blistering skin and fever-clouded minds, or didn't return at all. Trade slowed, the town grew tense, and rumours of dark magic spread through the docks.

A wizard hiding on [[Widow's Rook]] was responsible, using the curse to hoard [[Spice]] shipments and disrupt commerce. Around the same time, a secretive group of [[Gnome|gnomes]] arrived in town — watching, quiet, and seemingly connected to something larger.

[[Faefi]], [[Picasso]], [[Ira]], [[Vel'Korr]], and [[Soffalie]] investigated and cleansed the town of the affliction. The gnomes' presence hinted at the beginning of the [[Gnome Exodus]].
