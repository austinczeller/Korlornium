# [[The Severance]]
## Description
Occurs on the 24th of [[Salaale (Quiet)]]. It is celebrated today as a festival of feats and ingenuity. Events commonly occurring at Severance Festivals include; Magic Shows/Competitions, Traveling Circus, and fight pits.

[[The Severance]] is an ancient religious holiday whose meaning has been lost over time.
It is believed through ancient [[Dithoria (God)|Dithorian]] scrolls that this falls around the time when [[The Pantheon of Major Powers]] exited [[Erodas]]. But this such meaning has long faded from common memory.
