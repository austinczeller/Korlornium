# The Divergent War
[[The Divergent War]] refers to the war which followed [[The Red Storm]]. It was named the [[The Divergent War]] because it politically separated [[Siroth]] and [[Caldrith]].

The war was between the religious entities of [[Dithoria City]], the militia of [[The Rellian Dynasty]], and the [[Siroth Empire]].

[[The Rellian Dynasty]] tried to control all of [[Korlornium]] through military force. It came to a conclusion as religious forces from [[Dithoria City]] pushed [[The Rellian Dynasty|Rellian]] troops out of [[Wyegate]] and united northern [[Caldrith]] under the [[The Righteous Banner]].
