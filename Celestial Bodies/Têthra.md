# [[Têthra]]

## Description
[[Têthra]], otherwise known as the [[Têthra|tidal moon]], is the closer white moon of [[Erodas]]. It completes a full lunar cycle ever 64 days.
This moon is responsible for tidal shifts around [[Erodas]] and often more associated with [[Tidemother]]. 
The moon has a sister [[Pêpra]], known to bring chaos and fear to folks around [[Erodas]].
