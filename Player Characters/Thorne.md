**Backstory**
Thorne is a young half-elf from a small cultist clan [[Heaven's Hole]]. She left the clan recently and ended up in [[Duskwind Landing]] on her [[One-trek]].

Thorne was raised as a collective and has several sisters and brothers, not blood related. She has parents ([[Darter]] and [[Moon]]) but is not particularly close with them as they weren't the central figure of her upbringing.
