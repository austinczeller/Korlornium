# Kel
**Player**: Kevin
**Class**: Paladin (2) Barbarian (1)
**Species**: Half-[[Orc]]

![[Kel.png]]
**Short Description**
Full name: Kel'og Sea'Ryel
A sad boy half-orc who's family used to own the [[Side Quest Tavern]] in [[Duskwind Landing]] which recently closed under his leadership. He is a paladin and barbarian who is always looking to protect and help out where it see's fit. He is good buddies with [[Horace Greentusk]]. Thinking about opening a coffee cart. Used to give out quests at side quest.


**Backstory**
A tavern owner that he inherited from his parents and follower of [[Dithoria (God)]] (Artisan background). Although large and intimating in stature he is a softy at heart and wants to protect people close to him or those he considers allies. He hasn't quite sworn an oath yet because he's still unsure whether he truly believes in everything Dithoria stands for. When he rages its more of an emotional and angry-fuel anxiety attack. He doesn't like himself when he rages but knows it's for the safety of others - he just wishes he didn't have to rage to help people.


**Personal Quest**
- Help and protect others and be useful
