# Ira
**Player**: Rachel
**Class**: Monk
**Species**: Half-[[Elf]]

**Short Description**
Ira is a young half-elf woman who escaped a nefarious boarding school, [[The Silvertree Conservatory]], for half-elves to teach them elvish culture (similar to NA residential schools). Ira was forced to learn the arts of aristocracy and elegance along with all the other young women, while her brother also attended and learned martial arts. Once they escaped he taught Ira self defense. 

She is a monk and helped rid [[Duskwind Landing]] of [[Sea Rot]]. She left the group shortly after rumors is that she went cliff diving off of [[Widow's Rook]].
