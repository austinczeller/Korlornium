# [[The Heart of Parsus]]
## Description
The heart of Parsus is the manifestation of [[Parsus]]'s love.

At the spawn of mortality [[Parsus]] grew fond of the mortal resilience. 
Un-ascended [[Parsus]] created art and his last piece of art was a stone tablet, crafted from [[Ulthite]], or rather [[Ulthagos]] himself. This piece was said to be his last before he ascended to [[Yygarissil]]. As he knew his time on [[Erodas]] would not last as he was destined for ascension wept for years creating many of the ancient rivers. He fell in love with mortality in particular a mortal elvish artist named [[Eotanna]]. [[Parsus]] wanted to relinquish his divine prowess and pass it onto her to prolong her mortal life as she was dying. She saw the turmoil she was putting him in and decided to let go.

It is actually not the tablet but the instrument of his creation. paintbrush/chisel
