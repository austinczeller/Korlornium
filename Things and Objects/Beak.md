# Beak
> “Take [[Beak]]... Let her guide you." — [[Varnak]]

Beak is a [figurine of wondrous power](https://www.dndbeyond.com/magic-items/5413-figurine-of-wondrous-power) owned by [[Vel'Korr]] which resembles a raven.
Beak is often let out to 'play' and is treated like a companion or sibling to [[Vel'Korr]].
