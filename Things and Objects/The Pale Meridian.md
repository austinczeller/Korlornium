# The Pale Meridian

A sleek black-hulled brigantine captained by [[Viros]]. She moves with unnatural efficiency — no orders called, no noise from the crew, just the sound of wind and water. Those who watch her from the docks tend to look away quickly without knowing why.

## Crew

The Pale Meridian is crewed entirely by the reanimated dead. They work in unison, adjusting rigging and manning lines without a word or signal between them — like a single organism wearing many bodies. Among them are several former members of [[The Red Talons]], killed during [[Viros]]'s destruction of the crew near [[Frostcrag Glade]]. They wear the tattered remnants of their old coats. They do not speak. They do not rest.

## Notable Features

- **Black hull:** Lacquered dark and faintly reflective, the hull absorbs light rather than catching it. She is harder to spot at night than she has any right to be.
- **No name on the stern:** The ship carries no identifying markings beyond the hull colour.
