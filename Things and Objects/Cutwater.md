# [[Cutwater]]
## Description
[[Cutwater]] is a magic dagger owned by [[Granite]] of [[Heaven's Hole]]. He may give this dagger to [[Vel'Korr]] depending on the conversation. 

It appears as a rusty filet knife with a salt stained leather handle. Not rusted bits shine with a aquamarine blue.
