# Gate Stone
[[Gate Stone|Gate Stones]] are what are used to keep [[Dithoria (God)]] and [[Tiamat]] within the extraplanar dimension. 
The following gods were entrusted with [[Gate Stone]]s to keep the seal in tact. These were the most powerful deities at the time.
- [[Forgas]]
- [[Ulthagos]]
- [[Parsus]]
- [[Solwyn]]
- [[Sibuna]]
- [[Alune]]
- [[Tidemother]]
