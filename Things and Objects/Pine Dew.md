# Pine Dew
[[Pine Dew]] is a recreational drink commonly harvested in the early mornings in [[Heaven's Hole]].
It is often served by [[The Community at Heaven's Hole]] at recreational events such as [[Drop Party|Drop Parties]] or celebratory ceremonies.

Locals believe the drink captures the “first sigh” of the forest — the mingled breath of morning mist and otherworldly whispers.

It appears as a bright blue viscous liquid and is served in birch cups.

The drink is traditionally squished in the mouth and spat out, the taste is unpleasant for most. When spat out the hallucinatory visuals affect the user and they get the an advantage on insight checks.
When swallowed you get disadvantage on insight checks but get True Sight for one minute. True sight might just be that there is fey whispers or sprites on the Heavens hole folks.
