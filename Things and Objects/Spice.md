# Spice
Spice is a drug which is a common in the fields of piracy. It is a deep orange powder which is smoked in a pipe.

### Effects
**Duration:** 1 hour
- Resistance to psychic and necrotic damage.
- Immunity to the **Frightened** condition.
**Drawbacks:**
- Disadvantage on Intelligence and Wisdom checks.
- After the effect ends, make a DC 12 Constitution saving throw. On a failure, you are **Poisoned** for 1 hour.
