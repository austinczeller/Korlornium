# Bumbleberry Pie Recipe
This [[Bumbleberry Pie Recipe]] was passed down to [[Flint]] from his great grandmother. He serves it at the [[The Soggy Bottom]]. He told [[Soffalie]] after a bit of persuasion under the condition that she can not tell anybody.
It consists of:
- Butter
- Bumbleberrys
- Flour
- [[Stormwood]] syrup
