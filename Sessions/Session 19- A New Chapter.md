# Scene: Return to Stormveil
[[Stormveil]]
[[Rick Augerland]] and [[Numa]] welcome them back.
The Soggy Spa is just starting.
There is a free transit shuttle to the docks.

There is an [[Stormy Creaturez Nature Emporium]]

### Scene Type
Short vignettes, no meetings

### Give each PC ONE choice:
- Help build the Soggy Spa
- Test the Stormwood armor
- Intimidate old Terrance loyalists
- Gamble, drink, or recruit

Resolve each with **one roll + consequence**.

### World Movement

While this happens:

- A ship leaves for Thalmyre
- A courier arrives from Frostcrag Glade
- Someone mentions Nilah’s brother in the north
- A street preacher argues that _collective ownership is heresy_.

No council. No speeches. Motion only.

# Scene: Ship Readiness

### Option A — **Stormveil Refits (Safe, Slower)**

**Downtime-based, but gamified.**

They need **3 Upgrade Points** to be sea-ready for Thalmyre.

They gain points by:

- Spending gold
- Calling in favors
- Personally helping (skill checks, small scenes)

**Upgrade Examples:**

- Reinforced hull (+1)
- Storm rigging (+1)
- Armored rudder (+1)

**Complication Roll (1d6):**
- 1–2: Labor strike delays work
- 3–4: Sabotage attempt (quick scene)
- 5–6: Rumors spread about their destination

# Encounter: **The Brineknife Crew**

Low-ranking pirates who _think_ they’ve found easy prey.

**Twist:**

Examples:

- Intimidation via cannon fire (no hits)
    
- Ramming maneuver check
    
- Boarding threat that collapses instantly
