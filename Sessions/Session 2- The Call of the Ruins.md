#### Act 1: Setting the Stage (Duskwind Landing)

- The [[Gnome]] Presence → The players notice more gnomes in town, lingering longer than usual. They’re tight-lipped but show an interest in the forest and ruins.
    
- [[Vel'Korr]] & [[Borin Duskrag|Borin]]'s Dealings → Their negotiations could serve as background drama (maybe tensions rise, maybe Borin seems uneasy).
    
- [[Father Elias Vance]]’s Warning → If the party ignores the gnomes, Father Vance hints at something strange—the gnomes are avoiding the forest, which is unusual. He mentions the ruins and lost explorers.
    

### Act 2: Journey to the Ruins

- The ruins are deep in the forest, built atop an ancient underground tunnel system.
    
- As they approach, strange symbols of Ulthagos appear—carved into stone, half-buried statues, roots shifting as if breathing.
    

##### Encounters/Obstacles

- Ambush? → Gnomes loyal to the old ways try to stop them.
    
- Sinkholes & Quakes → The land itself is unstable.
    
- Echoing Whispers → The ruins seem to "remember" something—perhaps the players hear voices or see visions of the past.
    

### Act 3: Dungeon Crawl – The Lost Tunnels

- The ruins contain the entrance to an ancient temple to Ulthagos, where an old relic keeps Duskwind safe from sinking into the sea.
    
- The Lost Adventurer, [[Zora Meadowlark]] is here → He acts friendly, warns them of dangers below, but is secretly here for the relic.
    
- Deeper in, the party finds evidence of magical siphoning—crystals drained of power, earth magic fading.
