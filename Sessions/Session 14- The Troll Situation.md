## Recap
> Kel meets up with the party since arriving in Stormveil and working on his Stormwood Armor. He befriended Flint from the Soggy Bottom and fixed the place up a bit. He learned how to use his skills as a carpenter and and with a bit of musical arcane bring objects to life. He was generally unaware of the election but followed a bunch of mill workers to the election in the town square. Upon arrival Terrance successfully charmed him and he came to when the battle had finished. After much deliberation with the rest of the party, they convinced the town to hold a second election at the Soggy Bottom this evening. They conversed with Rick from the Millers Union and they invited the party plus Numa to their meeting this afternoon. At the meeting they discussed alternative economic opportunities, such as the Soggy Bottom Mud Spa, Owlbear Safaris, and a trade route to Duskwind Landing for Stormwood. These idea resonated with the people and Rick offered his support to Numa to unite the people of the region. At the election Numa ended up narrowly winning after finding a doppelganger shapeshifting multiple times to cast votes for Terrance. Upon recount Numa and Rick were victorious and Terrance and Sharron were run out of the tavern. Throne amused the crowd with a little bit of political standup and the gang set their sights on Heavens Hole after threats by Terrance that the people need to leave. The gang departed with Sofailiee's trusty owlbear friend in toe. They ended up at an unexpected location of a broken bridge and encountered a Troll and an Ogre. They attacked and this is where we left off....

## Scene: Dream Quest
Party takes a sip of the [[Heaven's Hole]] waters.
the sun goes dim and out of the water rises a clear sphere of water. It then will shift into a memory, roll initiative. before the initiative roll the figure shifts into the image which the party member just described. They get an action to do somethin

> "The water remembers… and it tests not one, but all."


When a PC drinks, have them roll a d6 and describe briefly:
1. **Joy** – A moment you felt pure happiness. Who were you with?
2. **Loss** – A time you lost something or someone important. How did it change you?
3. **Shame** – A mistake you still carry. What did you do?
4. **Love** – A person or place you cherished. Why was it so important?
5. **Rage** – A moment your anger burned out of control. Who or what was it directed at?
6. **Fear** – A time you were powerless or terrified. What haunted you?
