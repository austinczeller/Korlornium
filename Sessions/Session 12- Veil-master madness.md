## Scene: Aftermath of the Assassination Attempt

The failed (or partially successful) assassination **completely reshapes Stormveil’s political landscape**. Here’s how things play out depending on survival:

---

### **Case 1 — Terrance Survives, Numa Is Wounded** _(Most Likely)_

- **Immediate Effect:**
    
    - Terrance **claims the moral high ground**, presenting himself as the man who “survived an attack orchestrated by Heaven’s Hole.”
        
    - His supporters rally harder than ever.
        
    - Locals demand justice and security — hostility toward Heaven’s Hole skyrockets.
        
- **Impact on Numa & the Party:**
    
    - Numa is forced into hiding; her clan becomes politically isolated.
        
    - She begs the party to **help clear her name** before Terrance drives her people out of Stormveil.
        
- **Zora’s Goal:**
    
    - With tensions high, Heaven’s Hole becomes more vulnerable to infiltration, giving Zora a clearer path to the **Gate Key**.
        

---

### **Case 2 — Numa Survives, Terrance Is Killed** _(Dramatic Twist)_

- **Immediate Effect:**
    
    - Numa inherits immense **political pressure** — half the town thinks her clan murdered Terrance, the other half fears instability and unrest.
        
    - A smaller faction within Stormveil sees this as an opportunity to push for _severing ties_ with Heaven’s Hole entirely.
        
- **Ripple Effect:**
    
    - The players will need to **convince the townsfolk** of the doppelganger plot or risk open conflict.
        
    - Zora thrives on this chaos and begins using her agents to **fan distrust** between Stormveil and Heaven’s Hole.
        

---

### **Case 3 — Both Candidates Survive** _(Tense & Balanced)_

- **Immediate Effect:**
    
    - A **stalemate** ensues. Suspicion is everywhere, and paranoia spreads fast.
        
    - Stormveil becomes divided; rumors spread of “spies” and “traitors.”
        
- **Potential Side Plot:**
    
    - Someone plants forged evidence “proving” Numa ordered the hit.
        
    - The party must investigate and expose Zora’s manipulations before the situation spirals into civil unrest.
