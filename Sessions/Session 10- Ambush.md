## Recap
> The crew made preparations to leave [[Duskwind Landing]]. However, they realized they needed a crew in order to operate the [[The Salty Bitch]]. They first approached [[Morris Greentusk]] who agreed to join and possibly recruit [[Timmy Horton]]. Morris gave them the book [[Water Vessels for Fools]] which gave the crew a structure to their search. They next chatted with [[Felix Kled]] who agreed to join if he could take them to get more [[Stormwood]]. Felix was also contracted to carve a figurehead, [[Vel'Korr]] requested a raven. [[Kel]] also talked to [[Felix Kled]] about creating [[Stormwood]] armor which he was super stoked about. [[Soffalie]] chatted with [[Shandra Loomens]] to see if any druids wanted to come along. [[Shandra Loomens]] was unsuccessful but gave [[Soffalie]] a pendant of speak with animals. After some festivities the party was excited to set sail. That night they had a slumber party and found some interesting "godly" rocks, very brown but exuded divine energy. When [[Vel'Korr]] touched the rocks he felt [[Ulthagos]]. This was [[Vel'Korr]]'s first time back on the water since 15 years. Once at sea, [[Picasso]] felt a sinking feeling. The first night at sea [[Picasso]] saw a white squid with familiar eyes staring at him and he tried to follow. Later that night he had a dream where he followed the squid into an underwater cavern where [[Sliver]] turned into the squid and it ate his TMNT brethren. It then turned to him and said I AM SO HUNGRY. Picasso woke up sweaty. That morning the awoke to a thick fog and came across a island that seemed to be on fire. On shore they spotted a familiar bone snake... This is where today's adventure begins.

## Scene: Entering Stormveil
> As you emerge from the forest there you are greeted with the sound of rushed footsteps sloshing through the mud. You see a few hurried individuals wearing these brown hooded poncho looking garments they appear to be heading towards the nearest awning as they make their way down the street. You notice that you have emerged in what seems like a small neighborhood. There are a few residential looking houses who yards back up onto the forest. You see one house has fastened a tire swing to a tree, the rope looks worn and green. You see that many of the  houses have smoke emitting from their chimney and you can smell the comforting smell of wood burning in a fireplace. As you kind of wander past these modest houses you notice many of them are have these beautiful deep cherry shingles covering the  roof. You watch as the rain slides off these shingles and into a series bamboo-like funnels which guide the rain into the ground. These houses appear to be mostly log cabin style with small and sparse windows. You notice a few houses have small signs by their doors you read one and it says "Highbrow For Veil-father". There is a great big [[Stormwood]] tree in the center of an opening, surrounding it are a few benches. You see what you think might be the only stone in the entire town placed at the tree's twisted feet.
