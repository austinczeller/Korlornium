![[Gibberling_slug.png]]
This  slug is birthing the gibberling slugs.
The party can destroy the slug but it will emit a terrible screeching sound if hit. Gibberlings will come to aid if so, they are easy enough to dispatch. 
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bxoRRobHtGM&list=PLtdXajwkBeaUqspXbthJkim-l0_-k3gxU&index=60
##### Scene type:
Combat
##### Setting: 
Purple crystalline room
##### Music options:
- *Links to music*
## Setup
The chamber trembles. The slug pulses, birthing larval gibberlings that crawl blindly along the walls.
## Descriptions
As you approach you hear the sound of a thousand distant voices. 
There are a bunch of new born gibberlings lining the walls. And in the centre there is a ungulating purple mass. This is a massive slug (Milo sized). Two large cloudy purple eyestalks slowly scan the room, and you see a trail of purple slime covering all the dry surface. There is a large concentration of this glowing rock in this room.

### Environmental Mechanics

On **initiative count 20** (lair action style), roll 1d4:
1. **Birth Surge**: 2d4 gibberlings spill out
2. **Psychic Screech**: WIS save or disadvantage next action
3. **Crystal Pulse**: Void crystals grow difficult terrain
4. **Wet Collapse**: Ceiling drips acidic slime

### Player Options (Make these explicit)

- Kill the slug (harder fight)
    
- **Cauterize** it (fire, radiant, acid)
    
- **Seal it** (collapse tunnel, magic, brute force)
    
- **Harvest** it (gross but lucrative)
    

### Rewards (Choose based on approach)

- **Void-Silk Gland** (crafting, potion ingredient)
    
- **Ulthite Shard** (advantage vs Void Rot later)
    
- **Larval Compass** (points toward other Void activity)

# Scene: Road Encounter: Gnomes in Trouble

This ties directly into your **gnome exodus + ley line plot**.
### Scene: Broken Cart on the Breathway

### Setup

On the road away from Heaven’s Hole, the party sees:

- A **gnomish stone-cart** overturned
- One wheel crystallized purple
- Three gnomes arguing in frantic whispers

As the party approaches, the ground _ticks_.  
Like something counting down.

### The Gnomes

- **Etrin Coilthumb** – Ley-line surveyor
- **Maela Rootspark** – Botanist carrying saplings
- **Pell Underwhistle** – Apprentice, bleeding from the nose

They were mapping **safe ground** when the earth… shifted wrong.

### The Problem (Interactive)

A Void fault is opening under the cart.

Every round:

- Ground sinks
- Crystals grow
- Reality flickers

### Player Options

- Stabilize the ground (magic, tools, nature)
- Pull the cart free
- Evacuate the gnomes
- Seal the fault (hard but big consequences)

### Reward for Helping

The gnomes recognize Hollowreach immediately.

> _“That bow… that’s breathing wood.”_

They give:

- **A Gnome Ley-Token**  
    Once per session, ignore difficult terrain caused by magic or environmental effects.
- A rumor: _“If Heaven’s Hole collapses, it won’t scream. It’ll sigh.”_

This plants dread and curiosity.
