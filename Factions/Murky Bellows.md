# [[Murky Bellows]]
## Description
The [[Murky Bellows]] are a classic piracy gang. They appear welcoming at first and willing to help *at a cost.* 
They are one of the founding members of [[The Hydra (Syndicate)]], along with the [[The Stray Dogs]].

They are led by the smooth talking Captain, [[Hitchcock 'Goldentooth' Derose]]

The [[Murky Bellows]] operate out of [[Thalmyre]] and control many of the trade routes out of [[Dithoria City]] through [[The Scar]].

Many wealthy merchants recommend them as they are mostly honest when they are fucking you over. 

> "If you're going to get fucked, at least have a say in how"
