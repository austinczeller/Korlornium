# The Righteous Banner
[[The Righteous Banner]] is the name of the military brigade rallied during [[The Divergent War]] blessed by [[Dithoria (God)]] to keep his kingdom safe. Ultimately, [[The Righteous Banner]] defeated much of the [[The Rellian Dynasty|Rellian]] troops at the [[Battle of Wyegate]]. [[The Righteous Banner]] currently still controls much [[Caldrith]]'s northeastern land, except for the free city of [[Konduit]].
Commanded by [[Hethul, The Righteous]] during battles.
