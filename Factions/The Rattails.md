# [[The Rattails]]
## Description
[[The Rattails]] are a new age piracy gang who are all about explosions and are a bit unhinged. You never know what will happen..
They are led by their wild and very young leader, [[Tompkins 'The Squealer' Longtooth]]

They recently joined the [[The Hydra (Syndicate)]] by exploding the previous groups' headquarters, [[The Barnicle Boyz]].
