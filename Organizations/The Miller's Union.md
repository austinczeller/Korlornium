# The Miller's Union
[[The Miller's Union]] is a group of people working at the [[Stormwood Mill]] in [[Stormveil]] who are advocating for a worker owned mill. They have been enlightened by [[Picasso]]'s words and realize that the bourgeoise have been controlling the working class and profiting off of them. They wish to put the means of production back into the workers hands to workers.
