A species of intelligent humanoids present in coastal regions around [[Erodas]], however fairly uncommon. They commonly have bluish pale skin and appearances often mollusks and clams and starfish on them.. Often large in stature.

It is believed that these individuals are spawned from the love between the [[Tidemother]] and [[Alune]]. Legends state that [[Alune]]'s sister [[Sibuna]] granted the couple the gift of a child which is now known as [[Sea-folk]]. 
In [[Korlornium]] many people believe that [[Sea-folk]] are the undead souls of those who died in [[The Great Wave]] and are fairly common in areas around [[The Scar]].
