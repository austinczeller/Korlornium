# Dragon

[[Dragon|Dragons]] are ancient, long lived beings who dwell in [[Erodas]]. Originating on the large, sparsely populated continent [[Dracofold]], they have now spread to much of the globe. Temperament of this species are highly variable, but many mortals fear [[Dragon|dragons]] as they are typically extremely powerful. 
[[Tiamat]] is considered the eldest and most fearsome dragon of all time, although much of the histories surrounding this ancient dragon have been erased from mortal knowledge.
