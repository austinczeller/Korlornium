# # [[Sterrak Gauge]]

## Description
Legend states [[Sterrak Gauge]], befriended a stray dog who nursed him back to health after he was abandoned by his former crew. Once recovered and fueled by vengeance the two of them sought after his old crew, single handedly sinking their ship and killing everyone onboard.

[[Sterrak Gauge]] was the founding member of [[The Stray Dogs]], one of the most prominent pirate gangs in [[The Scar]]. In life Sterrak was the most fearsome swashbuckling red [[Dragonborn]], his name was known throughout [[Korlornium]]. He took particular interest in  [[Siroth|Sirothian]] artifacts including MAGIC ITEM, MAGIC ITEM.

He originally plundered the [[Tidemother]] [[Gate Stone]] along with many relics. Clues to their whereabouts are kept with the heads of the [[The Hydra (Syndicate)]]
