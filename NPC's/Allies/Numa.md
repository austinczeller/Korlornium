# Numa
![[Nuna 1.png]]

[[Numa]] is a human woman currently residing in [[Stormveil]]. She recently left [[The Community at Heaven's Hole]] and is looking to protect their way of life by running for Veil-Mother against [[Terrance Highbrow]]. She wants to protect [[Heaven's Hole]] from logging

She is out of her element in [[Stormveil]] but is passionate about helping others.

She has won the [[The 33rd Veil-Master Race]] and partnered with [[Rick Augerland]] and the [[The Miller's Union]] to ensure economic prosperity in [[Stormveil]].
