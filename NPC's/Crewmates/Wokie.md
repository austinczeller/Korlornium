# Wokie

[[Wokie]] is a [[wok-wok]] which were befriended on [[Saltmire Cay]] by the druid [[Soffalie]]. [[Soffalie]] convinced [[Wokie]] and [[Tokie]] to come aboard [[The Salty Bitch]] and be apart of the crew.

[[Wokie]] is a can be distinguished from his brother [[Tokie]] by the high energetic nature and thirst for destruction. [[Wokie]] is a bit more fluffy and has pointed ears.
