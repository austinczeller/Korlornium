# Felix Kled
[[Felix Kled]] is a old ass [[Dwarf|Dwarven]] woodcarver who the party met in [[Duskwind Landing]]. [[Felix Kled]] set up his shop at the [[Duskwind Fish Market]] and [[Kel]] purchased some [[Stormwood]] from him looking to create a set of armor out of the material. [[Felix Kled]] agreed to join the crew of [[The Salty Bitch]] as long as they took him to [[Stormveil]] so that he could replenish his stock of [[Stormwood]].
