## Description

A tall intimidating tough [[Orc]] and leader of the [[Murky Bellows]]. Legend states he defeated a [[Dragon]] wishing to claim [[Thalmyre]] with nothing but a club. He wears simple garb with ornaments shaped from black dragon bone. On his head sits decorative [[Dragon]] horns and has many rings and jewelry made with red [[Dragon]] scales.
