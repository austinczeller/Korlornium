# Zora Meadowlark
![[Zora_Meadowlark.png]]
## Description
Zora is a creepy yet unimposing [[Drow]] necromancer with an air of quiet intensity. Her pale white skin gives her an almost ghostly appearance, contrasting sharply with the deep green tunic she wears—its fabric old but well-kept, adorned with faint embroidery depicting twisting vines and skeletal hands. A large, ancient tome is strapped to her side, its cover weathered and bound in dark leather, the pages brimming with knowledge of life, death, and the in-between.

Her round glasses sit precariously on the bridge of her nose and her short wiry black bowl cut, gives her a studious, almost unassuming look—until one meets her eyes. Behind the lenses, her pale gray irises seem to stare through people rather than at them, as if constantly sizing up their souls rather than their words. Her movements are deliberate and eerily silent, and even in conversation, she speaks in a voice just a touch too soft, forcing people to lean in slightly—a subtle trick that makes even the bravest adventurers uneasy. Eerie long tongue.

She is the elder sister to [[Sibil Meadowlark]].
