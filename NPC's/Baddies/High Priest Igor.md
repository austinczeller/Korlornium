# High Priest Igor
## Description
A middle aged human male. He has deep brown scruffy hair with long sideburns with connect to a wolverine style beard. His face is twisted in a perpetual from. 

High Priest Igor holds a leadership position in the church of [[Dithoria (God)]].
Very authoritative. 

He will be involved with [[Zora Meadowlark]]'s plot to resurrect the champion of [[Dithoria (God)]]
## Relationship to party
- He had set [[Faefi]] out on a mission to recruit talented paladins and knights to the church.

### **Cantrips** 
- **Sacred Flame** (Dex save DC 15): Target takes **2d8 radiant damage** on a failed save. Ignores cover.
- **Word of Radiance** (Con save DC 15): Each creature of your choice within 5 feet takes **2d6 radiant damage** on a failed save.
- **Resistance**: Touch, target adds **1d4 to one saving throw** within 1 minute.
- **Spare the Dying**: Touch, stabilize a creature at 0 HP.
- **Light**: Touch, object sheds **bright light in a 20 ft radius** (and dim light 20 ft beyond).

### **Level 1 Spells** (4 slots)

- **Guiding Bolt** (Ranged spell attack, +7 to hit): **4d6 radiant damage**, next attack against target has **advantage**.
- **Healing Word** (Bonus Action): Heals **1d4+4 HP** (range: 60 ft).
- **Inflict Wounds** (Melee spell attack, +7 to hit): **3d10 necrotic damage**.
- **Purify Food and Drink**: Instantly neutralizes poison/disease in a 5 ft cube of food/drink.

###  **Level 2 Spells** (3 slots)

- **Hold Person** (Wis save DC 15): **Paralyzes humanoid** (1 min, concentration), save at end of each turn.
- **Zone of Truth** (Cha save DC 15): **15-ft radius**, creatures can’t lie while in zone for 10 min.
- **Continual Flame**: Creates **permanent flame** that sheds light as a torch (doesn’t burn).


###  **Level 3 Spells** (3 slots)

- **Animate Dead**: Raise up to **2 skeletons or zombies**, under your control for 24 hours.
- **Dispel Magic**: Ends one spell of 3rd level or lower automatically. Roll for higher levels (DC = 10 + spell level).
- **Tongues**: You (or target) can understand/speak any language for 1 hour.
- **Daylight**: 60 ft radiant sphere of light (bright as sun), 1 hr.
- **See Invisibility**: See invisible creatures/objects (1 hr).
- **Fireball** (Dex save DC 15): **8d6 fire** damage in **20-ft radius**
