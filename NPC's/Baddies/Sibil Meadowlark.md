# Sibil Meadowlark
![[Pasted image 20260119212141.png]]

A tall [[Drow]] with playful murderous intent. She has a crest of [[Sibuna]]. She is constantly smiling and never  blinks.

She has been infected by [[Void Rot]] and limps on her right leg.


Her eyes are black and reflect your image as you stare into them. Wiry black hair  shapes her face in an uncanny way to make her appear even younger. Her blouse appears to be stained red and you notice a belt holstering numerous knifes dripping with a thick red liquid. Her jagged pointed teeth are exposed  as she gnaws on some sort of bone like an animal picking every last piece of flesh clean off it. 

She is a blood hunter. maledict 

[[Sibil Meadowlark]] is the sister of [[Zora Meadowlark]] and was born in [[The Underdark]]. 
She has been traveling with the [[Dithoria City|Dithorian]]-born half-orc [[Gork]] for quite some time.
