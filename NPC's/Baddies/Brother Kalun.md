# Brother Kalun

A red [[Dragonborn]] monk who follows the god [[Dithoria (God)]]. He was a former pit fighter but found salvation in [[Dithoria City]] when he met [[High Priest Igor]].

He is traveling as a bodyguard to [[High Priest Igor]].
He was tasked by [[Zora Meadowlark]] to steal the ship's ledger back from [[The Salty Bitch]]. As they do not want word to get out they are involved with atrocities associated with [[Frostcrag Glade]] and the wizard [[Viros]].
