# Nashirra

[[Nashirra]] is a [[Bone Naga]] spitefully indebted to the necromancer [[Zora Meadowlark]].

Long ago, [[Nashirra]] was a revered guardian naga who watched over a sacred temple buried deep in the jungles of the [[Pugent Delta]]. Her duty was eternal vigilance — to protect ancient seals that kept darker powers at bay, including echoes of [[Dithoria (God)]]'s fractured essence. Over centuries, her sacred order faded, and the temple fell to ruin. But [[Nashirra]] remained.

Driven mad by solitude and corrupted whispers in the void beneath the world, she broke her oath. In punishment, she was slain and cursed — transformed into a Bone Naga, her soul bound in undeath to the very place she once swore to protect.

It was [[Zora Meadowlark]] who found her crypt, delving deep in search of the forgotten sigils binding [[Dithoria (God)]]. Rather than destroy [[Nashirra]], [[Zora Meadowlark]] restored her mind, carving away the madness like a surgeon, and freed her spirit from its cursed binding — but only partially. [[Nashirra]] still remains undead, her soul coiled in debt and black magic.

In gratitude, and with a deep, ancient loyalty to the draconic gods of old, [[Nashirra]] swore herself to [[Zora Meadowlark]]’s cause. She now serves as both guardian and advisor, twisted bone and old magic incarnate — dreaming of a world where the gods return and her curse is finally lifted.
