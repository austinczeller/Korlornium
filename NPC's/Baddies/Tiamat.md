# Tiamat
Tiamat is the working name of an ancient [[Dragon]] mother who was active in the [[Erodas]].
Upon arrival in [[Korlornium]] she was sealed in the center of what is now [[The Scar]] via the [[The Ethereal Chain]], [[Dithoria (God)]] sealed himself within this prison in order to keep her restrained as the gate could only hold back so much power. At the time it was land the location of the prison was underneath land (The Scar had not yet formed). [[The Great Wave]] was an attempt to reinforce the seal. Recently Tiamat has broken the mind of [[Dithoria (God)]] and is now posing as [[Dithoria (God)]] to get his followers to break her out.
