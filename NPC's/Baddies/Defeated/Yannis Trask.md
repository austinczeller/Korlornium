# Yannis Trask
![[Yannis_Trask.png]]
## Description
Appears as gruff middle-aged male [[Dwarf|dwarven]] sailor with ties to [[Dithoria City]]. He walks with a limp and has a great white beard. His eyes are black and he wears a long tattered blue coat and a ratty blue toque upon his head. He has one gold tooth you can see when he smiles. A symbol of the [[Tidemother]] hangs around his chest. He befriended [[Father Elias Vance]] to get information about [[Old Duskwind]] from him. He is the previous captain of [[The Salty Bitch]]. His true identity is a wraith controlled by Zora.

## Relationship to party
- Is ordered to dispose of them by [[Zora Meadowlark]] if they start telling the gang if they spill the beans about her.
- He is looking to hunt the party on [[Saltmire Cay]] for sport
## Notable Relationships
- Working with [[Zora Meadowlark]]
- Spent lots of time at the [[Tidemother Temple of Duskwind]] and befriended [[Father Elias Vance]] in order to learn more about the spirituality and history of worship in [[Duskwind Landing]].
- Apart of his crew is a former member of the [[The Red Talons]], [[Joanos]]

## Location
Was sent to scope out [[Duskwind Landing]] as there may be godly artifacts in the midst. Discovered and alerted [[Zora Meadowlark]] 

## Motives
- kill the party and bring [[Zora Meadowlark]] information about temples and ruins in [[Duskwind Landing]]
