# Joanos
![[Joanos.png]]
A human wizard formally apart of [[The Red Talons]]. He died in [[Frostcrag Glade]] by the  hand of the wizard [[Viros]]. We was later resurrected by [[Zora Meadowlark]] and put to work on [[Yannis Trask]]'s crew sailing [[The Salty Bitch]] across [[The Scar]] to find ancient relics and ruins.
In his current state he remembers little of [[The Red Talons]] and [[Vel'Korr]] and is indifferent and emotionless. He carries with him [[Joanos' Journal]] where he journals life events

## Relationship to party
- Used to be friends with [[Vel'Korr]], but is now indifferent.
