# Piamat
[[Piamat]] is a mischievous fey entity who is tied to the disappearance of [[Kel|Kel's]] parents. Desperate to find a way to enhance their dried oatmeal served at the [[Side Quest Tavern]], [[Kel]]’s parents struck a deal with [[Piamat]].

[[Piamat]] promised them a cow with milk so delicious it would revolutionize their dried oatmeal recipe. However, in exchange, he asked them to complete a seemingly simple task: break up with his girlfriend, [[The Night Queen]], a powerful and enigmatic Fey ruler.

Due to the distorted nature of time between the [[Feywild]] and the material world, what seemed like a brief errand has kept [[Kel]]’s parents away for years, leaving the tavern abandoned and shrouded in mystery.

## Personality

[[Piamat]] is a cunning and playful trickster who enjoys making mortals dance to his whims. His motivations are often selfish and petty, but his power and charm make him a dangerous force to reckon with.

## Abilities

- Manipulation of time within the [[Feywild]]
- Creation of enchanted creatures or items
- Power over minor illusions and glamours

## Motives

- To sever his relationship with [[The Night Queen]] without facing her wrath himself.
- To toy with mortals for his own amusement.
