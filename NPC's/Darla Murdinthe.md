 # Darla Murdinthe


## Description
A tough-love [[Sea-folk]] bartender of the neutral bar [[Whelk's Landing]] located on [[The Seat of Thalmyre]]. She is feared and respected among pirates.

[[Darla Murdinthe]] prides herself on having each isle of [[Thalmyre]] represented in her bar, [[Whelk's Landing]].
