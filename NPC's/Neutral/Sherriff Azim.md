# # [[Sherriff Azim]]


## Description
[[Sherriff Azim]] is a stout halfling man who wears a colorful orange and blue poncho. He has a thick moustache. Adorned on his head is an conical hats. His grin is wide and but his eyes are serious. 
He is very protective of his town and his kitties. The fluffy brown and rusty orange Manchon cat [[Georgey]] never leaves his side.
