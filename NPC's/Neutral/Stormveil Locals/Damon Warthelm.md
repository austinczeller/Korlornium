# Damon Warthelm
[[Damon Warthelm]] was a large bodied older Dragonborn and the 32nd Veil-father of [[Stormveil]]. 
He was found dead [[Sharron Fulton]] in the [[Permitting Office of Stormveil]].
He was notably complacent, and general sentiment was he was a lazy oaf. He was quite found of parades.

He was murdered by an assassin,  hired by [[Terrance Highbrow]].
