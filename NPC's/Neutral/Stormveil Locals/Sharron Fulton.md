# Sharron Fulton
[[Sharron Fulton]] is the middle-aged elven woman with pale white skin long pink fingernails and bouffant. She works at the [[Permitting Office of Stormveil]] and was the person who found the late 32nd Veil-father, [[Damon Warthelm]]. 
She is holds conservative views and wants peace and quiet in [[Stormveil]], she thinks this can be achieved by increasing logging so people have work and there will be less hardship. She works with her intern [[Victoria Zhao]].
