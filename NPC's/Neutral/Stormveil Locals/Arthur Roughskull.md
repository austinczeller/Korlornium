# Arthur Roughskull
[[Arthur Roughskull]] is a human entrepreneur residing in [[Stormveil]], brother of [[Torren Roughskull]]. He has attempted many business ideas, currently is renting out weird little pyramid rental home.
Lanky human male with greased back hair and a thick fuzzy black caterpillar mustache.
	"Join the triangle famalia!!!!!"
They are cheaply made pyramid homes which he rent to you for a discount and you can pay off your mortgage by selling another unit to other people and begin a pyramid scheme.
