# Morty Irvine
[[Morty Irvine]] is a mid-aged [[Fowlmen]] barbarian. 
He is short and features resemble a squat eagle his eyes are piercing and beak looks as though it is in a perpetual frown. 
He was raised in [[Stormveil]] by his foster parents.
[[Morty Irvine]] was injured at his workplace, [[Stormwood Mill]] and his feathery hand is warped and twisted.
He blames management for pushing him to hard and noticed that the mills' atmosphere really changed once [[Terrance Highbrow]] arrived at town. He is best bud's with [[Rick Augerland]] and apart of the [[The Miller's Union]].
