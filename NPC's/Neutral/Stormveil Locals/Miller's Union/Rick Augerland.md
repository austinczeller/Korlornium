# Rick Augerland
[[Rick Augerland]] is a tall gold [[Dragonborn]] lumberjack wearing blue and black plaid. He has a MOM tattoo. 
[[Stormveil]] born and raised.

Married to [[Victoria Zhao]]
He is the leader of the [[The Miller's Union]] a rouge group of workers rights advocates working at the [[Stormwood Mill]] in [[Stormveil]]. 

He is vice-Veil-master, partnered with [[Numa]] as of the [[The 33rd Veil-Master Race]]
