# Brick Ford
[[Brick Ford]] is a absolutely ripped [[Dwarf]] woman with thin wet sandy blond hair her face is clean shaven but as the party looks closer they see a bit of a five o'clock shadow. Her eyes are sunken and  [[Picasso]] spoke to her and [[Rick Augerland]] and they acquired arms from [[The Salty Bitch]] for [[The Miller's Union]]. 
She has spent the last year in [[Stormveil]] working at the [[Stormwood Mill]] and hates the sexist management as she spends too much time at the front desk as she is a woman.

She is originally from the [[Agite Mountains]]
