# Nixz
![[Nixz.png]]
## Description
[[Nixz]] is a lanky [[Goblin]] who is a regular at [[The Soggy Bottom]] in [[Stormveil]]. He has dark slicked back hair, a tattered flannel and square scratched up glasses. He works at the [[Stormwood Mill]] doing the accounting. As he is sitting there he is chewing on his nails. 
He is often drinking alone and always gets a Brambleberry Pie. He is mustering up enough courage to ask out the bartender, [[Flint]].
