# Nilah
![[Nilah.png]]
## Description
[[Nilah]] is a good aligned storm [[Dragon]] who watches over [[Stormveil]] and the [[Pugent Delta]].
Her and her kin have been chosen to guard the [[Parsus]] [[Gate Stone]] as they were to believed to fear the great dragon [[Tiamat]]. Her nest is located on the [[Scalecrest Bluffs]]. 

A piece of the [[Gate Stone]] was stolen by [[Deepseer Poppy]] but [[Nilah]] does not know about this.
