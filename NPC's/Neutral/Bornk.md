# # [[Bornk]]


## Description
[[Bornk]] is a tough and dumb dwarf ready to show off his skills. A thick tough looking dwarf wearing a heavy thick iron helmet covering most of his dirty soot covered face. As he struts forward you hear the chains of his chainmail softly cling against his metal gauntlet, like keys jangling. he coughs and snorts and spits.

He is travelling throughout the [[Agite Mountains]] looking for work as his previous employer was killed. He was his bodyguard.
