# Moon
[[Moon]] is the birthmother of [[Thorne]] and a 'Deepseer' of [[The Community at Heaven's Hole]]. Most of her figure is concealed through her long wavy brown hair which goes down to her feet. She speaks in slowly and deliberately. She left [[Heaven's Hole]] on her [[One-trek]] to visit the lands of [[Siroth]] and often talks about the harshness and dryness of the area. 

She sits as an advisor to [[Deepseer Poppy]].
[[Moon]] is not close with [[Thorne]] as she did not raise her. However, [[Moon]] is very much concerned with the quality of her [[One-trek]].
