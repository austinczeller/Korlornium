# Tragdar Windkeeper
Is a [[Dragonborn]] male residing in [[Heaven's Hole]]. His scale shone with a marvelous golden hue and he wears simple linen tunic that is immaculately white. His eyes shone with the same gold as his scale. Near his neck and his left side of his shoulder his scales are mutilated like he was burned, a token of his [[One-trek]] leaving him mute.

[[Tragdar Windkeeper]] is the youngest and most recent 'Deepseer' of [[The Community at Heaven's Hole]].
