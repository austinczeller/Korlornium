# Granite
[[Granite]] is a cool guy from [[Heaven's Hole]]. He officiates [[Drop Party|Drop Parties]].
He is a tall but thick boned pale skinned [[Elf]]. He wears a light well loved linen tunic with a green rope tied around his waist. His hair is a mop of sandy blond, and you see two friendly golden eyes peaking out behind the scruff. He does a lot of winking.

Has a bad relationship with [[Vel'Korr]] and [[Kel]] as they tried to strong arm him to fork over more money than he has.

Runs a non-profit animal rescue.
