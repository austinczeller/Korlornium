# Darter
[[Darter]] is a tall older spindly Half-[[Elf]] with sharp features. His short sandy blonde hair comes together at the back in a pretty lengthy rattail. His shirt is made up of brownish linen which is two sizes too big and is patched together in various places. His hands are stained purple at his fingers. 

[[Darter]] is the birthfather of [[Thorne]] and lives within [[The Community at Heaven's Hole]].
He ventured into [[Heaven's Hole]] once his [[One-trek]] was complete and had [[Thorne]] shortly thereafter with his wife [[Moon]]. 
Although not close with [[Thorne]] as [[Darter]] did not raise her he has a curiosity about her whereabouts.
