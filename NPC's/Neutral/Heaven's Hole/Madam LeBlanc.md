# Madam LeBlanc
![[Madam_LeBlanc.png]]
[[Madam LeBlanc]] is a gender fluid [[Feywild|Fey]] entity. Who resides in [[Casa LeBlanc]]. They are a collector of all things "Mundane". 

They are a tall faun-like creature with long shaggy fur, they carry a gnarled and knotted wooden staff with tassels and bobbles hanging from the ends. Long flowing blue and orange robes flow from their furry human-like shoulders and cover up most of he their waist. They move erratically in sudden lurching movements as if you were trying to walk down the plane aisle in heavy turbulence. Their eyes are shine with an iridescence and notably are two different shade of peculiar orange and red
