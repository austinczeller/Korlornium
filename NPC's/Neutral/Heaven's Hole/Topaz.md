# Topaz

[[Topaz]] is a muscular grey-furred [[Tabaxi]] with patches of white and black splotches across his body. You notice there is area where his fur is less dense and at some points the pale skin is visible indicating a hard life.

Most of his body is covered by a brown thatched poncho. A long crooked tail peaks out of the bottom of the fabric and is dragging across the ground.
He is a deepseer in [[Heaven's Hole]] and holds a leadership role in the [[The Community at Heaven's Hole]]. 
On his [[One-trek]] he traveled to the [[Agite Mountains]] and spent many years traversing the mountains and returned to [[Heaven's Hole]] after escaping the clutches of a yeti.
