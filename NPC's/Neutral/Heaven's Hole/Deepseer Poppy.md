# Deepseer Poppy
[[Deepseer Poppy]] is the elder of [[Heaven's Hole]]. Myra is a [[Elf]] with long black dreaded hair going past her waist with colorful feathers, and ribbons woven in. Her skin is a rich ebony tone that has looks relatively unweathered despite her supposed age. Her eye shone the same brilliant gold as Thorne and much of [[The Community at Heaven's Hole]], however, the irises have almost whitened. Appearing as though her whole eye is an iridescent golden color. **Her arm is withered and scarred from frostbite.**


She is the leader and wisest member of [[The Community at Heaven's Hole]].

She is one of the few individuals who have survived plunging to the depths of [[Heaven's Hole]] and entering the portal to the [[Feywild]].

During her [[One-trek]] she stole a shattered piece of a [[Gate Stone]] from the dragon [[Nilah]] and gave it to the [[Madam LeBlanc]]. She believed that in taking the a piece of the stone she would bring power to her people.
