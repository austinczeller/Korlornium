# Regi Clasphand
![[Regi_Clasphand.png]]
[[Regi Clasphand]] is a young red [[Tiefling]] rogue who is built like a sly and doesn't say much. He has a toothy grin and one horn is broken.

He is super eager and thinks everything is AWESOME!


**HP:** 26  **AC:** 14  
**Attacks:**

- **Rapier:** +5 to hit, 1d8+3 piercing; +2d6 Sneak Attack (once per turn)
    
- **Hand Crossbow:** +5 to hit, 1d6+3 piercing; +2d6 Sneak Attack if eligible
    
- **Cunning Action:** (bonus action) Dash/Disengage/Hide
