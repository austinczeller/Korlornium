# Alain Clasphand
![[Alain_Clasphand.png]]
## Description
Alain is a young adventurer and leader of [[Clasphand's Hammers]], a traveling group of orphaned children turned adventurers. Alain is proud and loyal, everything he has learned is self-taught so he has a very one-dimensional view. Trying to look heroic
His parents were miners in the [[Agite Mountains]]. They traveled from mine to mine searching for work as they were not apart of the [[Copper Hand]].  They were last seen near [[Frostcrag Glade]].

The wizard [[Viros]] has enslaved the flesh of Alain's parents to mine a specific and dangerous ore, [[Ulthite]].
